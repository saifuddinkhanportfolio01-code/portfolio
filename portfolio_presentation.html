01. Discovery & Wireframing

Every project starts by understanding user pain points. I map out early user flows and map structural wireframes on paper to iterate rapidly before touching digital design tools.

This phase ensures information hierarchy aligns perfectly with business objectives and technical scoping constraints.

02. High-Fidelity UI Design

Translating validated wireframes into responsive, pixel-perfect UI designs. I establish scalable design systems with reusable components, clear typography hierarchies, and strict WCAG accessibility standards.

Designs are built ready for smooth, seamless handoff to engineering teams.
